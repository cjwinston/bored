About Us!

Bored is an initiative intended to improve the daily lives of all those struggling to find fun, engaging activities. Many of us have refrained from going out much during the pandemic and now that things are looking up, it's hard to change our homebody habits. Our website is designed to help with this adjustment by providing quick access to events near you, as well as helping you fight boredom with movies and shows to watch and trivia games to play!
